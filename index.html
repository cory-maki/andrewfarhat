About Andrew

Teaching the Gospel in a distracted city

Andrew Farhat is a Denver pastor and teacher whose ministry centers on a simple conviction: in the midst of distraction and misplaced hope, people can walk together toward renewed hope and life in Jesus Christ. That mission shapes everything he does, from Sunday teaching to the small groups that meet in homes across the region.

He serves with a strong teaching church rooted in the historic St. John's congregation, the oldest Lutheran church in Denver, and helps lead its Highlands campus, Renewal Church, planted in 2018. His work spans ministries for singles, families, and the retired, and he is part of the team training everyday missionaries through the Spiritually Vibrant Homes course.

You can read more about Andrew's approach to teaching, discipleship, and outreach on his pastoral ministry in Denver, or get in touch directly with a question about his ministry.
